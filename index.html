How it helps

Three ways it gets you ready.

One companion for the whole road to the oral — organize, rehearse, and close your gaps.

Case-list companion

Your list, exam-ready

Import your de-identified ABOG case list once. OBMOB organizes it by content area and keeps it ready to drill — no retyping, no PHI.

Voice-AI examiner

The real exam, on demand

A neutral examiner questions you out loud, one step at a time — anchors on your case, branches to test breadth, and never tells you you're right.

Adaptive coaching

Know exactly what to fix

After each case: a specific scorecard with the model answer, plus delivery notes — pace, pauses, confidence. Your weak areas drive what you drill next.
