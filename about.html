About

Built by a practicing OB/GYN, for OB/GYNs.

OBMOB is a voice-AI mock oral-board examiner that drills you on the half of the ABOG oral that matters most: your own de-identified case list.

Why we built it

The ABOG oral certifying exam is unlike anything else in medicine. Half of it is built from the de-identified case list you submit, and you defend your real decisions under questioning. Yet most preparation is expensive, scheduled around someone else's calendar, and generic. Synthetic scenarios you will never actually present.

OBMOB was created by a practicing OB/GYN who lived that gap. It reads the de-identified list you already prepared for ABOG, organizes it by content area, and examines you on the cases you will actually defend, on demand, as many times as you need, the night before or three months out.

What makes it different

  • Your cases, not generic ones. Every other AI prep tool drills synthetic scenarios. OBMOB examines you on your real, de-identified list.
  • A demanding, realistic examiner. A neutral voice examiner that probes your reasoning, changes the variables mid-case, and does not coach you during the exam, like the real thing.
  • Specific, guideline-anchored feedback. A scorecard that grades what you actually said against the standard, on the spot, not a vague impression.
  • Clinical credibility. Designed and reviewed with a board-certified OB/GYN.

Our standard on safety

OBMOB processes de-identified information only and is not a medical-advice service. It's built with and clinically directed by a board-certified, practicing OB/GYN, and our clinical knowledge base is sourced to primary guidelines (ACOG, SMFM, and primary literature) and cross-checked against them. AI can make mistakes. This is exam practice, not patient care. Always verify clinical details against current guidance and primary sources.
