What the ABOG oral exam is

The answer-first block — the crisp, liftable paragraph an AI or a snippet can quote.

Short answer

The ABOG oral (Certifying) exam is a structured, ~3-hour spoken examination taken after the written qualifying exam. Candidates are examined across obstetrics, gynecology, and office practice — half on a list of their own collected cases, half on standardized structured cases — by boarded OB/GYN examiners who probe reasoning, judgment, and management out loud.
